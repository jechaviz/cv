# Project: Enterprise Electronic Billing
**Company:** Terapixel / **Tralix México** (Leading PAC)
**Period:** 2014 – 2021

### Project Context
Development of high-complexity billing solutions for corporate clients operating under the framework of **Tralix**, the pioneer Authorized Certification Provider (PAC) in Mexico.

### Objective
Develop and maintain fiscal compliance (CFDI) for massive commercial exchange in the retail and manufacturing sectors.

### Specific Responsibilities
* Build custom XML ***Addendas*** under the standards of major retailers (Walmart, Soriana, Costco, etc.).
* Implement digital signature engines and cryptographic validation in accordance with ***SAT*** Annex 20.
* Integrate the billing ecosystem with world-class ERPs.

### Achievements
* Technological enablement for hundreds of suppliers, allowing frictionless digital commercial exchange.
* Zero fiscal compliance incidents during massive billing periods.

### Technologies / Tools:
`Java` · `XML Architecture` · `Cryptography` · `Institutional Integration`
