# Project: AllCalls.io Feature Expansion & Integrations
**Client:** **AllCalls.io** (NY/WY) - InsurTech Platform
**Period:** Jun 2024 – Nov 2025

### Client Context
**AllCalls.io** is a platform that provides solutions to the life insurance industry by connecting licensed agents with prospects in real-time through live call transfers, optimizing the sales cycle.

### Objective
Extend the platform's capabilities by developing new features and integrating with critical third-party service providers for the operation.

### Specific Responsibilities
* Develop and implement new features that expand the reach of the ***AllCalls.io*** ecosystem.
* Integrate the platform with external service providers (telephony services, CRMs, and data verification tools).
* Collaborate in the continuous improvement of existing features, ensuring system scalability.

### Achievements
* Successful implementation of third-party integrations, expanding the platform's operational capabilities.
* Deployment of critical new features for the end user without compromising system stability.

### Technologies / Tools:
`Python` · `AsyncIO` · `GraphQL` · `REST` · `External API Linking` · `FastAPI` · `VoIP/CRM Integrations`
