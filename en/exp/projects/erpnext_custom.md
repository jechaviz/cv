# Project: ERP Ecosystem Integration (Frappe/ERPNext)
**Company:** Terapixel (Software Boutique)
**Period:** 2016 – 2021

### Project Context
Integration and adaptation of ERP systems for the comprehensive digitalization of operational processes in Mexican companies.

### Objective
Optimize operational continuity through the integration and extension of workflows on enterprise Open Source technologies.

### Specific Responsibilities
* Adaptation of tactical modules and alignment of business flows within **SAP**, **Oracle B1**, **Microsoft Dynamics**, and the **Frappe/ERPNext** ecosystem.
* Integration of mobile and web applications to extend operational flows.
* Connect the ERP with external messaging systems, banks, and third-party APIs (Tralix, etc.).

### Achievements
* Successful centralization of finances and operations through strategic integrations and workflow customizations across multiple ERP platforms.
* Improvement in operational efficiency through the comprehensive digitalization of processes with world-class software.

### Technologies / Tools:
`SAP` · `Oracle B1` · `Microsoft Dynamics` · `ERPNext` · `Frappe` · `Integration Architecture`
