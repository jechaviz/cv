# Project: Antamina Unified Chat Interface (AI)
**Client:** **Antamina** (Peru) - Global Mining Operation
**Period:** Apr 2024 – Sep 2024

### Client Context
**Antamina** is one of the world's largest polymetallic mines, located in the Peruvian Andes, with a high-tech operation requiring efficient information flows.

### Objective
Develop an intelligent and unified chat interface to optimize access to operational information, telemetry, and real-time incident management.

### Specific Responsibilities
* Design and implement the chat architecture using ***Amazon Connect*** for orchestration and ***Amazon Bedrock*** for Generative AI capabilities.
* Develop business logic and integration connectors through ***Amazon Lambda*** and ***Amazon API Gateway***.
* Integrate critical mine systems, including machinery telemetry, incident systems, and other internal operational processes.

### Achievements
* Centralized access to complex operational data through an intuitive conversational interface.
* Reduction in incident response times through direct integration with monitoring and alerting systems.

### Technologies / Tools:
`Amazon Bedrock (Generative AI)` · `Flutter` · `React Native` · `Amazon Connect` · `Amazon Lambda` · `API Gateway`
