# Project: Mobile Architecture and Consulting
**Client:** **DFX5** (Miami, FL) - International Software Factory
**Period:** Jun 2024 – Nov 2025

### Client Context
**DFX5** operates as a high-level software factory based in Miami, providing robust technological solutions for international clients across various sectors.

### Objective
Lead the consulting and architectural design of multiple mobile applications, ensuring a solid technical foundation aligned with business objectives.

### Specific Responsibilities
* Act as **Mobile Development Architect**, designing **end-to-end (e2e)** applications for various sectors.
* Define the technical ***Roadmap*** and perform ***LoE*** (Level of Effort) estimations to ensure project viability.
* Design deep technical architecture, including the **object layer**, interfaces, and system modularization.
* Simultaneously manage the architecture of several critical projects, ensuring quality and scalability standards.

### Achievements
* Successful definition of modular mobile architectures that allowed agile scaling of App features.
* Optimization of operational planning through precise LoE estimations and realistic roadmaps.
* Delivery of robust architectural designs that served as the basis for the full application lifecycle.

### Technologies / Tools:
`Mobile Architecture` · `Object Modeling` · `Interface Design` · `Roadmap Strategy` · `LoE Estimation` · `Multi-project Management`
