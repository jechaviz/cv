# IT Technical Manager
**Petco México** – Querétaro, México (Software Consultancy)
**October 2023 – April 2024**

### Company Context
**Petco México** is a sister company of **Petco** in the United States (1500+ branches) and functions as its dedicated software factory for the local and international market.

### Purpose of the Position
Build and manage the technology department, establishing the engineering culture, development processes, and the technical direction of the company from its initial scaling phase.

### Cross-cutting Responsibilities
* Lead and manage technical talent growth (2 architects, 2 leads, 6 developers).
* Establish technical direction and engineering best practices (**TDD**, **Clean Architecture**, **Agile Processes**).
* **Technological Governance:** Perform exhaustive code reviews for every production release and lead strategic pair reviewing sessions.
* **Crisis Management:** Lead **War Room** sessions for the immediate resolution of technical *stoppers* and critical production issues, actively participating in the execution of solutions via **ServiceNow**.
* **Stability Strategy:** Design and implement strategies for the drastic reduction of accumulated incidents, integrating historical technical debt resolution into the annual **Development Roadmap** and implementing scheduled tasks via **Cron Jobs** for preventive maintenance.
* **Technology Life Cycle:** Evaluate, decide, and implement the incorporation of new technologies and the decommissioning of obsolete systems.

### RELEVANT PROJECTS

#### Project 1: Store Employee App (Takeover & Modernization)
**Period:** Oct 2023 – Jan 2024 (3 months)
**Objective:** Gain full ownership and modernize the internal mobile application used by Petco employees for store operations management.
**Specific Responsibilities**
* Lead the technical transition of the **Store Employee App** (iOS and Android), covering critical functionality for **inventory**, **planograms**, and **deliveries**.
* Execute the technical knowledge transfer from the external vendor to the internal team within a critical 3-month timeframe.
* Resolve critical production issues through the backend in **Python (Flask)** and **AWS Lambda** services.
* Train internal staff in the mobility stack (**React Native**) and modern backend architectures (**Node.js**) alongside incident resolution.
**Achievements**
* Successful transition of 100% code ownership within the established timeframe.
* Stabilization and improvement of the mobile tools used by thousands of employees in branches.


#### Project 2: Strategic and Budget Planning (Annual Plan)
**Period:** Jan 2024 – Apr 2024
**Objective:** Develop and execute the organization's technical and budgetary roadmap.
**Specific Responsibilities**
* Collaborate closely with Product Owners and Stakeholders to define the Annual Development Plan.
* Prepare the technical and financial proposal for budget requests to the finance department.
* Organize the work agenda by prioritizing innovation, new developments, and the resolution of existing needs monitored in **ServiceNow**.
**Achievements**
* Annual budget approval based on a solid technical roadmap.
* Structured execution of the scheduled agenda, balancing new development with evolutionary maintenance.

**Technologies / Tools:**
`Python (Flask)` · `Node.js` · `React / React Native` · `AWS Lambda` · `Redis` · `ServiceNow` · `Cron Jobs`
